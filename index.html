MediaBridge

Rate movies and books to get cross-media recommendations

Welcome to MediaBridge! This project is currently in its data collection phase. Right now the app accepts movie and book ratings, which will be used to train a cross-domain recommendation model. Once the model is ready, you will be able to input your favorite books and get movie recommendations, and vice versa. Thank you so much for helping me build this. A few extra ratings from you personally makes a huge difference, and I genuinely appreciate every single one.

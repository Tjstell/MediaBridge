MediaBridge

Rate movies and books to get cross-media recommendations

Welcome to MediaBridge! This project is currently in its data collection phase. Right now the app accepts movie and book ratings, which will be used to train a cross-domain recommendation model. Once the model is ready, you will be able to input your favorite books and get movie recommendations, and vice versa. I would love it if you can rate at least 20 movies and books, if you could rate 40, that would be even better, and make a huge difference to the model. Thank you so much for helping me build this!
